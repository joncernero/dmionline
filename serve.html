serve

DMI is run by a small team of volunteers stateside, a minimally supported in-country team, and faithful sponsors. We've been blessed by so many partners over these 18 years — and with continued support, we'll keep growing. If you'd like to get involved, here are a few ways you can help.

how to help

give

Consider supporting DMI financially — whether through a one-time donation or a monthly commitment. DMI is a federally registered 501(c)3 and your gift is tax deductible.

Nutrition Program $24,000 / year

25 kg of posho and 15 kg of beans distributed twice a month to 18–20 families, every first and third Sunday. Includes the cost of delivery.

General Fund $15,000 / year

Covers medical needs, administrative costs, and direct family support — ensuring that donations made to our programs are fully utilized there.

Donate

host

Gather a group of friends for dinner, dessert, or cocktails and invite us over to share more about what DMI is doing and where we are going. We can meet in-person or via video chat — a chance to get to know you and answer your questions.

Email Us

volunteer

DMI is run by just a few volunteers stateside. Are you a content writer? Fluent in Luganda? Could you spare time to pack envelopes, or want to come to Uganda with us? Whatever your level of expertise, talents, or skills — we'd love to hear from you.

Get Involved

share

Find DMI on Facebook and Instagram using the links in the footer. Sharing our posts, pictures, and blogs with your network is an easy but effective way to help us get the word out.
